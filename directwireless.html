Seamless Alarm Monitoring with DirectWireless—Your Secure Connection Starts Here.

  • DirectWireless offers unparalleled reliability and security for alarm transmission, making sure your business or home is always connected to your alarm monitoring provider. With DirectWireless, you can trust that your alarms will always be heard, no matter the situation.

Why Choose Direct Wireless?
Always Connected: DirectWireless permanently links your alarm system to your monitoring service provider, ensuring that your system is always online and ready to report.
Regular Status Checks: Depending on your service plan, DirectWireless checks and tests your alarm’s connection every 90 seconds or 24 hours, providing peace of mind that your alarm interface is operating without interruption.
Instant, Secure Transmission: In the event of an incident, DirectWireless sends alarm messages instantly across our secure network, ensuring they are received and acknowledged by your central monitoring station for immediate response.

Key Features:
Compliant with AS2201.5-1992: DirectWireless meets Class 2, 3, and 4 alarm transmission system standards, ensuring compliance with industry-leading security requirements.
Universal Compatibility: Designed to interface with any standard alarm control panel, making it perfect for any installation, whether residential, commercial, or industrial.
Reliable Alarm Transmission: Ensures all alarm, status, and event messages are communicated instantly and securely.
Continuous Monitoring: With regular testing of your alarm system interface, any potential issue is identified before it becomes a problem.
Fast Incident Response: Alarm messages are instantly transmitted and acknowledged, ensuring swift action by your monitoring provider.
High-Level Wireless Security: The product offers superior wireless security, making it a reliable and robust solution for your alarm system needs.

Remote Access and Management:
Manage and monitor your DirectWireless alarm system from anywhere through the DirectWireless website portal or the smartphone app for both Android and iOS devices, providing ultimate control and flexibility.
